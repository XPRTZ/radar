<b>Cypress</b> is the next generation E2E testing tool that made itself famous by building its own architecture instead of using Selenium like most testing frameworks do. It's an <a href="https://www.cypress.io/how-it-works/">all-in-one test suite</a> that is blazing fast, easy to setup and a delight to develop with. That's what we like at XPRTZ so more than enough reason to assess it!
